The Future of Transportation

Flight Enabled's goals are as follows:

1. Steadily improve small UAVs through iterative software and hardware innovations until they can be trusted to be safe, quiet and capable package delivery vehicles a la Matternet.

2. As small UAVs are proven, iteratively make them bigger so that they can carry passengers and cargo safely and can be piloted as simply as cars.
